Jun Kato

Jun Kato is a senior researcher at AIST, where he works on Human-Computer Interaction research, more specifically, designing tools for programming interactive media content. He also serves as a technical advisor at Arch Inc., a Japanese animation production company, and leads Arch Research. He received a Ph.D in Computer Science from The University of Tokyo in March, 2014. Prior to graduation, he worked as research intern at Microsoft Research Asia, Redmond, and Adobe Research Seattle.

He has regularly gained academic recognition such as Microsoft Research Asia Fellowship 2012, ACM CHI 2013/2015 Best Paper Honorable Mention, and HAI 2014 Best Paper Nominee.
